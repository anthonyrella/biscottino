About Biscottino

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in a standard assorted platter?

A standard platter includes our five signature cookies:

Almond Biscotti

Classic Amaretti

Lemon Twists

Chocolate Chip

Shortbread

Can I customize my platter?

Absolutely! Every tray is made to order and tailored to your taste. Choose any or all of our signature cookies, and ask us how we can customize your flavours and designs. Pricing for custom platters begins at $75/kg.

How much does a platter cost?

An assorted platter starts at $75 per kilogram. One kilogram contains approximately 160 cookies. Let us know how many people you’d like to serve, and we’ll recommend a platter size.

Do you provide smaller packaging options for guest favours or corporate gifting?

Yes, we do! Contact us to discuss your preferred flavours, styles, and packaging options.

Do you deliver?

Free pick-up is available in Kleinburg. We offer delivery for an additional fee based on location.

How long can I store the cookies?

For optimal taste and freshness, store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature and consume within 3 days. For long-term storage, freeze in an airtight container for up to 3 months.

Do your cookies contain allergens?

Many of our cookies contain nuts, dairy, egg and/or gluten. All cookies are made in a kitchen that handles these ingredients. While we can adjust some recipes, we can't guarantee allergen-free cookies. If you have specific allergies or dietary needs, contact us to chat about possible customizations.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 1 week in advance for standard platters. For custom designs or large orders, please contact us 2-3 weeks ahead of your event to ensure we can accommodate your needs.
